At our company, we’re all about adventure and excitement. We’re big fans of the animated television series Adventure Time, and we love the way it combines humor, action, and heart. We’re inspired by the show’s colorful cast of characters and their epic quests through the Land of Ooo.


We’re also passionate about sword fights and dragons. There’s something thrilling about the clash of steel and the roar of fire-breathing beasts. We love to imagine ourselves as brave knights, battling fierce dragons and defending our kingdoms from danger.


But we’re not all serious all the time. We also love to have fun and play pranks on each other. One of our favorite ways to do this is by Rick Rolling each other. There’s nothing like the look on someone’s face when they realize they’ve been tricked into watching the music video for Rick Astley’s “Never Gonna Give You Up.”
